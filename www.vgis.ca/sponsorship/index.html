Canada Family Sponsorship

Sponsorship Overview

Canada offers family sponsorship programs for Canadian citizens and permanent residents to bring loved ones to Canada. Whether you're sponsoring a spouse, child, or parent, these programs help reunite families.

Check if you're eligible to sponsor or be sponsored for Canadian permanent residence.

Who can I sponsor to come to Canada?
  • Spouse, Common-law, or Conjugal Partner
  • Parent or Grandparent
  • Child or Other Dependant
  • Orphaned Brother, Sister, Nephew, Niece, or Grandchild
  • Other Relatives (in exceptional circumstances)
How can I be eligible to sponsor a family member?
  • You must be a Canadian citizen or permanent resident.
  • You must be at least 18 years old.
  • You must be able to provide for the basic financial needs of your family member for three years.
Cost to Sponsor a Family Member

The cost to sponsor a family member includes several fees. Below are the details:

  • Sponsorship fee: $75
  • Principal applicant processing fee: $490
  • Right of permanent residence fee: $515
  • Biometrics fee: $85
  • Total: $1,165
How Long Does Sponsorship Take?

Sponsorship applications typically take approximately 12 months to process. Processing times can vary depending on the specific case.
