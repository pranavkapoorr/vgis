Meet Our Team

Our team is dedicated to providing expert advice and guidance through the immigration process.

Dimple Verma

Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC# R708308)

Dimple Verma is the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) / Director at VGIS and a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC# R708308). Dimple moved to Canada as an international student in 2014 and has a personal understanding of the challenges faced by foreign students and workers who wish to make Canada their permanent home. He has gained firsthand experience of the Canadian immigration process and the importance of staying updated with the ever-evolving immigration policies.

Having volunteered as a student leader, Dimple helped guide his fellow students in choosing the right path for their immigration process. This experience ultimately inspired him to make immigration consulting his career, and today, he is committed to assisting others in navigating their Canadian immigration journey.
